# Mukhrai (Braj)

**Why it matters:** A quieter stop in the Nandgaon–Barsana belt, associated with simple, intimate lilas remembered by locals.

## How to visit
- Best months: Oct–Mar
- Timings: typically daylight hours; confirm locally
- Etiquette: modest dress, keep area clean, respect local seva

## Nearby darshan
- Nand Bhawan (Nandgaon)
- Ter Kadamb
- Barsana (Shri Radha Rani Temple)
